Salonathon is a home for underground, emerging, and genre-defying art!

Salonathon has been running every Monday at Beauty Bar since the summer of 2011. Hundreds of short performances have happened. There has been sweat, there has been real and fake blood, there have been stillettos, puppets, podcasts, dances, diaries, uncomfortable silences, roaring laughs, confessions, songs, swaths of exposed skin, stories, and all kinds of gloriously unnamable experiments.

Now nearing its 4 year anniversary, Salonathon shows no signs of slowing. There are so many exciting, hungry, charasmatic creators out there. There are so many ideas to try. I, for one, cannot wait to see what is next.

Jane Beachy, Artistic Director & Curator
